About

Link's origin really depends on which game you are playing. My first Zelda game was "Ocarina of Time," where he was essentially adopted by the forest folk and raised before heading off on his famous journery. Even though his origin changes, his goals and ambition generally remain the same; to save the world and of course, Princess Zelda from whichever form of Ganandorf that has reared it's ugly head to wreak havoc in the world.

Personality

Link is said to be the embodiment of a true "Hero" helping those in need around Hyrule as well as outside of it. He carries himself with a sense of Nobility, even though he wasn't necessarily raised as a noble in every game. Though he is the savior, he can also be quite the trouble-maker, breaking pots in peoples houses and messing with the local cuccos, eventually causing them to seek revenge and bombard him in a barrage of flying cuccos!!
