Attractions

Trails, trails, and more trails!!

Brantford has quite a few attractions that happen both seasonally and year-round. There are lush trails that go all throughout and around the city, either with trees framing them or the Grand River. The trails are also a part of the Canadian Rail Trails. Some of the trails are so thick that you can escape into nature without seeing a house or building through the forests. There is also a trail along the dike, a giant hill that was built to help prevent flooding from the Grand River, that connects Brantford to the neighboring towns Hamilton and Paris. There's Brantwood Farms that host strawberry picking, apple picking, and Halloween hayrides, pumpkins and haunted house for some family fun! There's also Earl Haig, a family waterpark and a carnival that comes seasonally right beside the waterpark.
