Brantford

Come for the casino, Stay for the cheap property tax!

Brantford, Ontario is a smallish town with a population of 104,688 as of 2021. Filled with many trails one can explore as well as many heritage sites and buildings for some local history. There are many murals dedicated to WWII throughout the city as well. The main one being centered in downtown Brantford. There are also things like Air Cadets and Sea Cadets for children to get a taste of military life. One of the local high schools still has their separate “Boys” and “Girls” entrances, kept showing history and the age of the building itself. Many of the buildings are considered heritage buildings, keeping the history alive within the community.
