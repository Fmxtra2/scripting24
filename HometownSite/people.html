People

Brantford has a very strong sense of community that is also very diverse. A lot of people move there because of the more affordable living for families. Having more property than some of the other towns as well as still being small enough to allow your children to walk around the neighborhoods to play with friends at local parks. Brantford also has a wonderful transportation system, being able to get to pretty much everywhere in town on the city buses without having to travel or wait for long. There are also quite a few family-run businesses that have been running for years and for some decades. Brantford continues to grow both geographically and economically.
