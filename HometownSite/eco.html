Economy


How money is made!

Brantford is very diverse in the community and the businesses that it has also reflect that, with key sectors that make up much of its economy. The key sectors being:

  • Advanced Manufacturing
  • Film, television, and digital media
  • Food and beverage manufacturing
  • Plastics and rubber products
  • Warehousing and logistics

Brantford has actually been one of the many filming locations for plenty of TV shows and movies. Silent Hill being one, as well as Murdoch Mysteries, The Umbrella Academy, Wysteria, Handmaid's Tale, Chucky (TV series), Locke and Key and that's just to name a few. There is also the casino, which is well known locally, as well as non-locally since it often brings in people from other towns as well for a fun-filled and hopefully lucky night.
